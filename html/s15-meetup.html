CS615A -- Aspects of System Administration - Spring 2015 - HW#N

HW#N: Attend a relevant Meetup/Talk/community event

Summary:

System Administrators constantly need to keep up with new technologies and development. One way to do this is to participate in the local tech community. New York has a vibrant tech scene with countless meetups and events and offers unparalleled opportunity to learn.

This "assignment" requires you to attend at least one tech event or meetup that is related to the profession of System Administration.

This assignment is worth 20 points.

Deliverables and Due Date

The deliverable for this assignment is a brief summary of the event, why you chose it, what you learned etc. You are to send this write-up to the class mailing list together with any relevant links.

If you are taking pictures at the event or wish to share any other larger documents, please make them available on your personal course website (ie under https://www.cs.stevens.edu/~your-username/CS615/.

The due date for this assignment is 2015-05-18 10:00.
