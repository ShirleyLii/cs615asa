Capture The Flag!

Level 0

The instructions for the next level are, of course, top secret. Hence, they can only be made available in encrypted form. Likewise, we want to ensure that only authorized parties have access to these instructions.

We accomplish this by using Pretty Good Privacy (PGP). If you don't know how to use PGP yet, maybe this link will help.

Your team needs to send an email encrypted with this key to jschauma@stevens.edu with a subject of "[CS615] level 0". In this email, you must note:

  • the name of your team
  • the Stevens usernames of all members of your team
  • the PGP key id(s) of all team members' public keys
    (if the public keys are not available on the keyservers, please also include them)

Each team may submit this email exactly one time in any 24 hour window. If not all the correct information is contained in the email or it is not properly encrypted, then that team must wait for 24 hours to try again.

Upon successful completion of this task, you will receive (encrypted) instructions on how to proceed to the next level.
